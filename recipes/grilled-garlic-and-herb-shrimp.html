Grilled Garlic and Herb Shrimp

Description

Grilled Garlic and Herb Shrimp is a delicious and easy-to-make dish that showcases the natural sweetness of shrimp with a flavorful garlic and herb marinade. The shrimp are grilled to perfection, creating a juicy and tender texture with a delightful aroma.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the garlic, parsley, basil, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make the marinade.
  2. Add the shrimp to the marinade and toss to coat evenly. Let it marinate for at least 15 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the shrimp onto skewers if desired.
  5. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they turn pink and are cooked through.
