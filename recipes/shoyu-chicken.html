Shoyu Chicken

Description

Shoyu Chicken is a popular Japanese dish that features tender chicken pieces cooked in a savory soy sauce-based marinade. The chicken is typically marinated in a mixture of soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar, which gives it a rich and flavorful taste. The dish is often served with steamed rice and garnished with green onions or sesame seeds.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 2 tbsp sake
  • 1 tbsp sugar

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar to make the marinade.
  2. Add the chicken to the marinade and toss to coat evenly. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill or pan-fry the chicken for 6-8 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.
